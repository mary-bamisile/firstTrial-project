OUR PROGRAMS

Women Techstars

The women techstars initiative is aimed at bridging the digital and technology knowledge divide between men and women as well as ensuring equal acess to opportunity for all. We are empowerind girls and women across Afican with varying degrees of digital, deep tech and soft skills required within technology and ecosystem.

The women techstars initiative will train 5 million across Africa by 2030

Emerging Market Model Initiative (EMMI)

The emerging market model will train 36,000 youth over a one year period in through a blended learning approach: online and sphysical through skill training and youth centers across Nigeraia. this will be ahieved through multi-year collaboration with four strategic federal goverment minitries and 12 state governments

Basic Digital Literacy For Southwern Nigeria

The BDL-SN is designed to reach 1000 beneficiaries in 10 rural clusters, across 10 state im southern Nigeria, targeting 50% vulnurable women and girls, 30% living with disabilities (PLWD), e.g mobility, speech, polio etc and 20% for other vulnurable groups.

The training will be implemented across the following sourthern states: Ekiti, Oyo, Akwa-Ibom, Delta, Edo, Rivers, Ogun, Abia, Enugu, Anambra.
